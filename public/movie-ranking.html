The Best Movies - Marie's Opinion

This is a list of my top 3 favorite movies!

Scott Prilgrim vs The World

I love the concept of this movie. The visual effects and the song Black Sheep, played by The Clash at Demonhead, was really catchy.

Pride and Prejudice

I am not fond of romance movies, but I would watch this masterpiece over and over again.

Encanto

I love musicals. Encanto is easily one of my favorite musicals written by Lin-Manuel Miranda. I also love a few of his other musicals such as Hamilton, In The Heights, and Warriors.
